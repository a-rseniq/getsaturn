Becoming Verified?

To get the Verified Plugin Creator on SaturnBypasser Discord, you must meet the requirements:

  • Over 20 reacted checkmarks on your plugin.
  • Been a member for around 1 month.
  • Having more than 3 plugins created.
  • Having no bad plugins.
  • Good reputation.
